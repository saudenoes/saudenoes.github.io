An idea in the head, a camera in hand and an on-screen knowledge

This is a project of interdisciplinary activity proposed by the disciplines of History, Philosophy, Geography, Literature and Portuguese Language, Sociology, English Language, Programming, Physical Education, as part of the semester evaluation for the students of the technician integrated in Industrial Automation and Informatics for Internet


General Objectives

To know the local reality through its historical, social, economic, political, cultural, linguistic, educational and scientific aspects, through the realization of audiovisual productions


    Specific Objectives

  1. Opportunities for educating technicians and knowledge related to audiovisual production
  2. Identify problems faced by the local population related to social and economic development
  3. Know the diversity of elements that make up the local culture
  4. To analyze the linguistic formation and the cultural influences that can be recognized in the local reality
  5. To know the forms of corporal expression, as well as, the aspects related to the public health that involves the practice of physical exercises
  6. Produce scripts that address historical, social, economic, political, cultural, linguistic, educational and scientific aspects of the local reality
  7. Produce audiovisuals with a view to holding a festival

Presentation, objectives and justification of the theme

Class: Computer Integrated

Group: Matheus, João Antônio, Sibely, Ingridy, Isaque e Maria Eduarda

Theme: Espírito Santo's Health



    Objectives:

  1. To address as a way to inform and criticize public health in the general state, with a major focus on the theme of depression. Showing that it is not just a "freshness" but a serious illness like any other.

  2. In showing the health situation in the state, we intend, in addition to informing, to make the population aware of its duty to collect from the public authorities their rights and investments.


    Justification:

  1. Our group decided to opt for health because it requires attention that is not properly offered, that few people have deep knowledge about, only know what is presented by traditional media, often manipulated.

  2. AWhen we see some events in the health of the state, we feel "touched" by the situations, and even if it does not reach a great repercussion, one of the justifications for choosing the theme is to do our part, and what is within our reach, so that this problem be solved.

  3. The topic depression was chosen because it is a very common disease in society today, especially with adolescents. Proof of this are cases of suicide in the game "Blue Whale" and cases of depression after the series that recently rocked, "13 Reasons Why"..
