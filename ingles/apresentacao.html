AUDIO VISUAL

Presentation, objectives and justification of the theme

  • Class: Integrado de Informática
  • Group: Matheus, João Antônio, Sibely, Ingridy, Isaque e Maria Eduarda
  • Theme: Saúde no Espírito Santo


    Objectives:

  1. To approach as a way to inform and "criticize" the disregard for public health in the state, citing:
    • Cases of negligence
    • Lack of remedies
    • Lack of materials
    • Very poor infrastructure
    • Disqualified professionals
    • Lack of money

  2. In showing the situation of health in the state, we intend, in addition to informing, to make the population aware of its duty to collect from public authorities their rights and investments.


    Justification:

  1. Our group decided to go for the health issue because it is a long-standing "problem" in our state, which few people have deep knowledge about, only know what is presented by traditional media, many times, Manipulated.

  2. When we see some events in health of the state, we feel "touched" by the situations, and even if it does not reach a great repercussion, one of the justifications for choosing the theme is to do our part, and what is Our scope so that this problem can be solved.
