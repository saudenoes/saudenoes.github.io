AUDIO VISUAL

Presentation, objectives and justification of the theme

  • Class: Computer Integrated
  • Group: Matheus, João Antônio, Sibely, Ingridy, Isaque e Maria Eduarda
  • Theme: Espírito Santo's Health


    Objectives:

  1. To address as a way to inform and criticize public health in the general state, with a major focus on the theme of depression. Showing that it is not just a "freshness" but a serious illness like any other.

  2. In showing the health situation in the state, we intend, in addition to informing, to make the population aware of its duty to collect from the public authorities their rights and investments.


    Justification:

  1. Our group decided to opt for health because it requires attention that is not properly offered, that few people have deep knowledge about, only know what is presented by traditional media, often manipulated.

  2. When we see some events in the health of the state, we feel "touched" by the situations, and even if it does not reach a great repercussion, one of the justifications for choosing the theme is to do our part, and what is within our reach, so that this problem be solved.

  3. The topic depression was chosen because it is a very common disease in society today, especially with adolescents. Proof of this are cases of suicide in the game "Blue Whale" and cases of depression after the series that recently rocked, "13 Reasons Why".
