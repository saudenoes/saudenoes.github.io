AUDIO VISUAL

An idea in the head, a camera in hand and an on-screen knowledge

This is a project of interdisciplinary activity proposed by the disciplines of History, Philosophy, Geography, Literature and Portuguese Language, Sociology, English Language, Programming, Physical Education, as part of the semester evaluation for the students of the technician integrated in Industrial Automation and Informatics for Internet




General Objectives

To know the local reality through its historical, social, economic, political, cultural, linguistic, educational and scientific aspects, through the realization of audiovisual productions


    Specific Objectives

  • Opportunities for educating technicians and knowledge related to audiovisual production
  • Identify problems faced by the local population related to social and economic development
  • Know the diversity of elements that make up the local culture
  • To analyze the linguistic formation and the cultural influences that can be recognized in the local reality
  • To know the forms of corporal expression, as well as, the aspects related to the public health that involves the practice of physical exercises
  • Produce scripts that address historical, social, economic, political, cultural, linguistic, educational and scientific aspects of the local reality
  • Produce audiovisuals with a view to holding a festival
